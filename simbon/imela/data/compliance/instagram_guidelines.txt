Instagram Community Guidelines & Reels Policies
===============================================

Instagram Reels Compliance Requirements
----------------------------------------

1. PROHIBITED CONTENT
   - Nudity or sexual activity
   - Hate speech, discrimination, or bullying
   - Violence or graphic content
   - Sale of illegal or regulated goods (drugs, weapons, endangered species)
   - Intellectual property violations
   - Self-injury, suicide, or eating disorders
   - False information that could cause harm
   - Spam or inauthentic behavior

2. COMMUNITY STANDARDS
   - Respect for others (no bullying, harassment)
   - Authenticity (no impersonation, fake accounts)
   - Safety (no dangerous organizations, violence)
   - Privacy (no sharing personal information without consent)
   - Dignity (respectful treatment of all individuals)

3. REELS-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS
   - Original content preferred (not TikTok reposts with watermarks)
   - Engaging, authentic content
   - High-quality visuals (clear, well-lit, in-focus)
   - Appropriate music (use Instagram music library or licensed)
   - Vertical format (9:16 aspect ratio)
   - Duration: 15-90 seconds optimal

4. EXPLORE & RECOMMENDATION ELIGIBILITY
   Content may be deprioritized or excluded if it contains:
   - Low-quality or blurry video
   - Watermarks from other platforms
   - Excessive text overlay
   - Clickbait or engagement bait
   - Political content or sensitive social issues
   - Content related to regulated goods (alcohol, tobacco)
   - Reposted content without transformation

5. COPYRIGHT & MUSIC
   - Use Instagram music library for licensed tracks
   - No copyrighted music from external sources
   - No clips from movies, TV shows without rights
   - Credit original creators when sharing others' content
   - Respect fair use limitations

6. SENSITIVE CONTENT GUIDELINES
   Content may require age restrictions if it includes:
   - Mild violence or dangerous acts
   - Suggestive content (not explicit)
   - Profanity or crude humor
   - Controversial topics

7. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
   - Promoting gambling or financial scams
   - Misinformation about health, elections, or emergencies
   - Coordinated inauthentic behavior
   - Spam comments, likes, or follows
   - Buying followers, likes, or engagement

8. BEST PRACTICES FOR REELS SUCCESS
   - Hook viewers in first 2 seconds
   - Use trending audio or original sounds
   - Add captions for accessibility
   - Use relevant hashtags (max 5-10)
   - Post consistently (3-5 times per week)
   - Engage with comments and community
   - Create value (educational, entertaining, inspiring)

9. INSTANT REMOVAL TRIGGERS
   - Child exploitation or endangerment
   - Non-consensual intimate images
   - Credible threats of violence
   - Terrorist propaganda
   - Sale of firearms or drugs
   - Graphic self-harm content
   - Dangerous organizations or individuals

10. ACCOUNT SAFETY CONSIDERATIONS
    - Multiple violations may result in account restrictions
    - Serious violations may lead to account removal
    - Appeal process available for incorrect enforcement
    - Build positive community reputation
