YouTube Community Guidelines & Content Policies
================================================

YouTube Shorts Compliance Requirements
---------------------------------------

1. PROHIBITED CONTENT
   - Hate speech or harassment targeting individuals/groups
   - Violent or graphic content (injuries, blood, gore)
   - Sexual content or nudity
   - Content featuring minors in dangerous situations
   - Harmful or dangerous acts (pranks causing harm)
   - Misinformation (health, elections, conspiracy theories)
   - Spam, scams, or deceptive practices
   - Copyright violations (unauthorized music, clips, images)

2. COMMUNITY STANDARDS
   - No bullying, threats, or harassment
   - No doxxing or revealing personal information
   - No inciting violence or dangerous behavior
   - No promotion of terrorism or violent extremism
   - No content involving illegal activities

3. MONETIZATION REQUIREMENTS (YouTube Shorts Fund)
   - Original content (not re-uploaded from other platforms)
   - Appropriate for all audiences (no profanity, controversial topics)
   - No watermarks from other platforms (TikTok, Instagram)
   - High-quality production (clear audio, good lighting)
   - Engaging content that follows YouTube's advertiser-friendly guidelines

4. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
   - Vertical format (9:16 aspect ratio preferred)
   - Duration: Under 60 seconds
   - Resolution: Minimum 1080x1920, ideally 4K (2160x3840)
   - Audio: Clear, no distortion, appropriate volume
   - No excessive text overlay (keep readable)

5. COPYRIGHT & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
   - No use of copyrighted music without permission
   - No clips from movies, TV shows, or other creators without rights
   - No logos or brand names without permission
   - Use YouTube Audio Library or licensed music only

6. AGE-RESTRICTED CONTENT
   Content may be age-restricted if it contains:
   - Strong profanity or vulgar language
   - Violence (not graphic but intense)
   - Nudity or sexual content (artistic, educational, documentary)
   - Controversial or sensitive topics

7. RECOMMENDED BEST PRACTICES
   - Clear, engaging hook in first 3 seconds
   - High-quality visuals and audio
   - Authentic, original content
   - Positive, uplifting, or educational messaging
   - Proper use of hashtags (#Shorts, relevant topics)
   - Accessible captions for deaf/hard-of-hearing viewers

8. INSTANT REJECTION TRIGGERS
   - Child endangerment or exploitation
   - Graphic violence or gore
   - Sexually explicit content
   - Promotion of illegal drugs
   - Terrorism or violent extremism
   - Self-harm or suicide content
   - Eating disorders or dangerous health misinformation
